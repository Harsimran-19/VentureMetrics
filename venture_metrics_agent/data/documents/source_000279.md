Sayuj is a start-up community network powered by the Software Technology Parks of India (STPI). The platform fosters an innovative ecosystem where collaboration and real-time knowledge sharing thrive. Start-ups gain access to a dynamic network, connecting with mentors, investors, industry experts, and other essential players. This platform acts as a catalyst for exchanging ideas and leveraging collective expertise to accelerate growth.
