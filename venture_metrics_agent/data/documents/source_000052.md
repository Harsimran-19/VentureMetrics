### Vision

[To develop Hong Kong as the world's leading Smart City, to foster knowledge economy, enhance quality of life and create a vibrant eco-system by using Information Technology and promoting more effective resource management.](/index.php/en/about-us/bg)

### Mission

|  |  |
| --- | --- |
