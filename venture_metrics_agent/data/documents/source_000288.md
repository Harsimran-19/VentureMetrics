**PRIME Database Group** is India’s leading provider of data on the capital market. We have developed several unique databases and websites (given below). We also provide a variety of other services such as database creation and management, content generation, website development and management, information consulting and data cleaning and standardization to other organisations.

By leveraging our deep understanding of information management, our clients are able to take better business decisions, improve their internal information processes and systems and also signiﬁcantly enhance their public information platforms.

Our clients include academic institutions, advertising agencies, asset management companies, audit firms, banks, corporates, credit rating agencies, custodians, debenture trustees, depositaries, developmental institutions, FPIs, financial institutions, hedge funds, HR firms, industry associations, insurance companies, investment banks, law firms, management consultants, media, NBFCs, PE/VC firms, PR agencies, printers, private banks, registrars, regulatory bodies, stock brokers, stock exchanges etc.

## Past Projects

### Databases

**_directorsdatabase.com_**

 This was a unique public service free website conceived and maintained by PRIME with the co-sponsorship of BSE. It provided at one place updated information on the directors on the boards of all actively traded Indian listed companies along with their detailed profiles, with an analysis and focus on independent directors. As on 31st March 2012, it had covered over 3200 listed companies and profiles of 14,621 individuals who were collectively holding 20,129 directorship positions. The website was inaugurated by Shri M.Damodaran, Chairman, SEBI in June 2006.

**Venture Capital Database**

 In 2001, PRIME had undertaken the very first authentic survey of VC firms on behalf of Thomson Financial. IVCA was also a partner to this project. This survey resulted in the production of the IVCA Yearbook 2001. This effort was subsequently repeated in 2002 and 2003, resulting in the production of the IVCA Yearbook 2002 and IVCA Yearbook 2003. The last Yearbook was launched by Mr. G.N.Bajpai, the then Chairman of SEBI at a function in Mumbai.

**themaexchange.com**

 This enabled sellers of companies to reach buyers and vice-versa. This database offered to the promoters, corporates and lenders, an organised, aggregated and pro-active platform to provide information on tap basis to potential acquirers in India and across the world on companies/ assets available for takeover/ strategic investment/ leasing. This website had unmatched features including hosting the offer of the seller until they found a buyer, also offering complete privacy.

**PRIME Private Equity & Venture Capital Database**

 Database providing details of Private Equity & Venture Capital Firms investing into India and Service Providers to this industry.

### Services

**_Investors Websites_**

 PRIME used to create, maintain, update and host Investors websites for listed and unlisted companies.

### Websites

**_iepf.gov.in- in English, Hindi and 11 Regional Languages_**

 An investor education, free public service website entirely conceptualized, executed and maintained by PRIME Database for the Investor Education & Protection Fund, Ministry of Corporate Affairs, Government of India. It was a free portal aimed at investor education with a focus on the capital market. The website (English version) was inaugurated by Shri Prem Chand Gupta, Minister for Corporate Affairs in September 2007. The website was then translated into Hindi and was launched by Shri Salman Khurshid, Minister for Corporate Affairs in March 2010. The website was also subsequently translated into 11 regional languages and launched by Dr.Manmohan Singh, Hon’ble Prime Minister of India in December 2010. In 2012, this website was handed over to the Fund for further operations.

**_dpsbhiwani.org_**

 Official website of the Delhi Public School, Bhiwani.

**_Investor Facilitation Platform for Department of Investment & Public Asset Management (DIPAM)_**

 The IFP was conceptualized keeping in view the overall objective of enabling an inclusive ecosystem that facilitates closer interaction amongst the Government, Companies & the investors on one hand & including investor feedback in better management of the Government investment in the companies on the other.

The platform was developed for all the constituents of the Disinvestment process to facilitate their engagement with the concerned parties in a more systematic and efficient manner. The platform also provided latest updates on the activities of the companies and investor presentations.

The IFP was a culmination of suggestions received from investors, investment bankers & other stakeholders during interactions in road-shows & other forums.

 Restructuring of Corporate and Member information databases at NSE and redesigning their presentation on the NSE website to make the information more usable/ user-friendly.The purpose of this project was to build better credibility of NSE as an entity which understands information requirements of its users and to build user loyalty.

**_Revamping the BSE website-www.bseindia.com_**

 Restructuring of databases at BSE and redesigning its website, including re-writing over 2,000 pages.

 As a public service, PRIME completed a redesign of the SEBI website, including rewriting of all pages, rationalization of grouping/headers/links, introduction of better tables/data, better navigation, faster searches, addition of new information etc. to make it more user-friendly (on a pro bono basis).

## Prithvi Haldea

Prithvi Haldea, born 1950, an M.B.A. (1971) from BITS, Pilani, is the Founder-Chairman of PRIME Database Group (www.primedatabasegroup.com), India’s premier database on the capital market since 1989. Between 1971 and 1988, he worked at senior positions in the corporate sector in the areas of exports, software, consulting, publishing, and advertising. He was also associated with the information industry and worked as a Consultant with The World Bank and with the U.S. Department of Commerce. Since 1989, when he founded PRIME Database, he has innovated several databases/websites on one hand and worked on corporate governance and investor protection on the other. **Ibaadat Foundation**

 Ibaadat Foundation is a charitable Trust founded in 2011 by Mr. Haldea to provide poets their rightful place in the society. This is done through staged musical dramas, which entirely focus on the literary and lyrical geniuses. In order to accomplish this, Ibaadat employs new formats. Rather than the conventional style which has predominance of songs, interspersed with some comments/shers, Ibaadat makes its shows far more interesting and informative, in a conversational manner. Nearly 65 shows covering 8 poets have already been held in New Delhi, Mumbai, Kolkata, Ludhiana, Jaipur, Udaipur, Agra, Bhopal and Dubai.

**Rekhta Foundation**
