## Notice to Users

1. This Image Mark Finder ("iMF") is an online search tool set up and maintained by the Intellectual Property Department of the Government of the Hong Kong Special Administrative Region ("IPD"). It seeks to provide an additional and specific avenue for search of comparable figurative trade marks (i.e. marks containing pictures/graphics/images) and composite trade marks (i.e. marks containing a combination of words/characters and pictures/graphics/images) in the register of trade marks ("register") kept by the Registrar of Trade Marks ("Registrar") under the Trade Marks Ordinance (Cap. 559).

2. All information accessible through the iMF sources from the records in the register which are accessible from the [Public Online Search](https://esearch.ipd.gov.hk/nis-pos-view/#/tm/quicksearch?lang=en) for trade marks ("POS") as set up and maintained by IPD.

3. The iMF should NOT be treated as a substitute for the POS given that the search results generated by iMF are intended for general reference only and may not be exhaustive enough to meet your own needs. Should you wish to obtain more comprehensive trade mark search findings, you are strongly advised to conduct separate searches in the POS by using proper device codes, trade mark texts or any other appropriate search criteria.

4. Neither should the search results provided by the iMF be regarded as an official opinion or advice rendered by the Registrar. You may, having regard to your own needs, request the Registrar to provide Preliminary Advice on Search of Records for a particular trade mark by submitting to the Registrar [Form T1](https://www.ipd.gov.hk/en/trade-marks/forms-and-fees/index.html#t1) with the applicable fee before filing an application for registration of the trade mark in Hong Kong.

5. The personal data accessible through the iMF as collected from data subjects is made available to the public solely for the purposes set out in the [Personal Information Collection Statement](https://www.ipd.gov.hk/en/privacy-policy-statement/personal-information-collection-statement/index.html) of IPD. The Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance, Cap. 486 ("PDPO") applies to the use of such data, including any subsequent disclosure or transfer of such data by any data user.

6. IPD reserves its right to modify, extend or suspend any search function of the iMF in full or in part, at any time and without prior notice.

7. The iMF is subject to a set of "Disclaimer" as specified by IPD from time to time.

8. Please **note** that by clicking the "Accept" button below for accessing any information through the iMF, you **confirm** that you have read and understood the aforesaid paragraphs, and that you:

a) agree to use any personal data that is accessible through the iMF only for the purpose(s) stated in paragraph 1.3(c) of the [Personal Information Collection Statement](https://www.ipd.gov.hk/en/privacy-policy-statement/personal-information-collection-statement/index.html) of IPD;

b) understand that use of such personal data for any other purpose or otherwise in contravention of the requirements under the PDPO is subject to enforcement action and sanctions under the PDPO; and

c) agree to abide by law in using the iMF and any information retrieved therefrom.
