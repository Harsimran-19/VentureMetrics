## Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Monster Resume Builder free to use?

Yes, Monster’s AI Resume Builder is completely free. You can build a resume from scratch or improve your existing resume with our professional templates and guided prompts.

There are no hidden fees, and you don’t have to enter credit card information to access your document. Once your resume is complete, you can download it for free in PDF or DOCX format.

You can create unlimited resumes for free and save them to your dashboard. Return anytime to continue editing and downloading your job application documents.

Monster also offers free supporting resources, such as career advice articles, sample resumes, and cover letter guidance to help you throughout your job search.

What should I look for in a resume maker?

The best AI resume builder will help you create an ATS-friendly resume quickly and easily. When choosing a resume maker, look for a tool that is easy to use, offers professionally designed templates, and lets you customize layouts, fonts, and sections.

Monster’s AI Resume Builder app combines all the [benefits of a resume builder](https://www.monster.ca/resume) into a single tool, helping you create a polished, recruiter-ready resume that stands out.

You can choose from a range of modern, professional templates suited to different industries and experience levels. It’s easy to personalize your template by rearranging sections or changing fonts and colors to match your style.

Powerful features, such as AI-generated content for key sections like your [resume summary](https://www.monster.ca/career-advice/resume/resume-summary-examples), make it easy to highlight your skills and qualifications effectively.

Are your resume templates ATS-friendly?

All of Monster’s resume templates are designed to meet best practices for applicant tracking systems (ATS), helping you [write a resume](https://www.monster.ca/career-advice/resume/how-to-write-a-resume) that ATS software can easily scan and parse.

ATS-friendly resumes are structured so that automated systems can read and interpret your information without errors. Key features that make our templates ATS-friendly include:

*   **Clean formatting**: Avoids complex graphics, images, text boxes, or tables that can confuse ATS software, ensuring your information is read accurately
*   **Standard section headings**: Clearly labeled sections like “Experience,” “Education,” “Skills,” and “Certifications” so the ATS can categorize your information correctly
*   **Readable fonts**: Simple [resume fonts](https://www.monster.ca/career-advice/resume/best-fonts-for-resumes) such as Arial, Calibri, and Times New Roman that are easy for both ATS software and human readers to scan
*   **Keyword optimization**: Incorporates industry-specific terms and relevant job description phrases to increase the chances your resume is recognized for the right roles
*   **Consistent structure**: Maintains uniform spacing, alignment, and bullet point formatting, making it easier for ATS systems to parse your information and avoid errors
*   **File compatibility**: Downloadable in ATS-compatible formats such as PDF or DOCX, ensuring your resume is readable by most applicant tracking systems

Monster’s resume templates and AI resume writer help you avoid common [resume mistakes](https://www.monster.ca/career-advice/resume/resume-mistakes-to-avoid) to create an ATS-friendly and recruiter-ready resume quickly.

Is your AI Resume Builder suitable for any experience level?

Yes. Monster’s AI Resume Builder is designed to support job seekers at every stage of their career, from students and entry-level candidates to experienced professionals and executives.

The resume creator offers guided prompts, AI-powered content suggestions, and plug-and-play templates that adapt to your experience level, helping you include the key [elements of a resume](https://www.monster.ca/career-advice/resume/resume-sections) that highlight your skills, accomplishments, and certifications.

Whether you’re creating your first resume or updating an extensive career history, Monster makes it easy to craft a polished, recruiter-ready document.

Is it easy to customize your resume templates?

Monster’s Resume Builder makes it simple to fully customize the design and layout of your resume on your desktop or mobile devices. It’s easy to tailor your resume for each job you apply to. You can tailor your resume for each job by highlighting the skills, [promotions](https://www.monster.ca/career-advice/resume/how-to-show-promotions-on-resume), and work experience that align with the employer’s needs.

You can easily adjust fonts, colors, spacing, and section order to match your personal style or the needs of a specific job. The intuitive resume editor tool lets you move headings and adjust layouts without any formatting issues making it easy to choose between a [one- or two-page resume](https://www.monster.ca/career-advice/resume/one-page-vs-two-page-resume) to showcase your most relevant qualifications.

You can add or remove sections to highlight your most relevant qualifications, such as promotions, [certifications](https://www.monster.ca/career-advice/resume), or measurable accomplishments. The flexibility of the resume generator helps you ensure every application is targeted and recruiter-ready.

Can I upload my new resume to Monster and connect with recruiters?

You can! Monster’s [resume upload](https://www.monster.com/profile/upload-resume) tool makes it easy to get your resume in front of recruiters and hiring managers. With over 14,000 companies using the resume database to connect with job seekers, uploading your resume can improve your chances of being seen by recruiters. Simply create a FREE monster account, upload your resume, and set your visibility in your profile. You can also choose to have recommended jobs sent directly to your inbox to quickly and easily apply to.

Can I download my resume in different formats?

Yes. With Monster’s Resume Builder, you can download your resume for free in multiple formats to suit any application or system. Options include PDF for a professional, polished look, DOCX for easy editing, and plain text (TXT) for online forms. You can download your resume as many times as needed and make updates or create new versions at any time, from any device.
