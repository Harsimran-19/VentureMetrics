# About T-Hub

About T-Hub

T-Hub is the world’s largest startup hub, supporting founders from ideation to scale through programs, partnerships, and global access anchoring some of the world’s most significant incubation and innovation initiatives.

It brings together startups, corporates, academic institutions, government bodies, investors, and global markets to support founders from ideation to scale. Through cohort-based initiatives across sectors, T-Hub enables founders to engage with leading mentors and access structured, stage-appropriate support. [...] T-Hub plays a distinctive role as an incubator of incubators. It anchors the AIC T-Hub Foundation, India’s leading Atal Incubation Centres within a national network of 70+ AICs supported by NITI Aayog. It also anchors India’s largest incubator for the defence open-innovation platform iDEX, hosts MATH India’s leading Centre of Excellence for AI and ML supported by the Department of Science and Technology and is home to India’s first Google for Startups Hub.

Spanning over 572,000 square feet, T-Hub provides founders access to world-class infrastructure designed to enable collaboration, experimentation, and scale.

Through these efforts, T-Hub strengthens founders to build and scale startups from Telangana for India and the world.
