The Knowledge Transfer Office is proud to spotlight the journeys of our most inspiring HKBU start-ups. This section goes beyond the business plan to share the real stories behind their success, celebrating everything from their first breakthrough to their latest achievements. Discover how these innovators overcame hurdles, reached significant milestones, and continue to grow. Be inspired by their accomplishments and learn from the tangible paths they forged within our community.
