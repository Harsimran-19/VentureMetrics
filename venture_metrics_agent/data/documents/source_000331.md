## Frequently Asked Questions

What makes Dealigence different?

Other platforms highlight static facts: funding history, HQ, and founding date.

Dealigence emphasizes **real-time dynamic signals**: workforce growth, go-to-market hiring, ARR estimates, product adoption and more while also providing the static facts.

Users detect companies earlier and with better context to capture deals first. Dealigence can be used stand-alone or as a complementary product to other platforms.

Where does Dealigence get its data?

Dealigence aggregates billions of data points daily across workforce, social media, news, app marketplaces, websites and more.

Every data point goes through our proprietary verification, hygiene, classification and inference models to maintain its statistical integrity with the highest standards.

We continuously refresh our data in real-time.

What’s the cost?

Flexible plans start at $67 / seat / week (billed every 4 weeks). You can cancel anytime.

Annual plans offer a discount depending on tier and consumption.

Wire transfers are supported in annual contracts. For further details, contact sales.

Can I use Dealigence for ‘CRM’ workflows?

Yes.

You can add notes, tasks, and statuses on companies.

Statuses also populate across all lists, enabling pipeline coverage views.

Contextual reminders can be set to resurface companies based on specific signals (senior hire, new market entry, funding and more).

Yes, substantial ones. Reach out to[startups@dealigence.vc](mailto:startups@dealigence.vc)for further information.
