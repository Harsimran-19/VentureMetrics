## Details

The UK innovation survey (UKIS) is the main data source for business innovation in the UK. The survey is conducted every two years. UKIS 2023 is the UK’s thirteenth survey of this type. It is used widely across government to help improve policy and by the research community for understanding the innovation landscape.
