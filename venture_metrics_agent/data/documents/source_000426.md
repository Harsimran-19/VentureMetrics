# I-Corps Impact Report: December 2025

Since 2012, the I-Corps program has had an incredible impact. For the states in our region, I-Corps is a catalyst to transform research into measurable, positive economic impact. Our December 2025 Impact Report celebrates and highlights the successes of I-Corps trained teams.

Read More:

[https://umcfe.my.canva.site/gl-icorps-impact-dec-25](https://umcfe.my.canva.site/gl-icorps-impact-dec-25)

### About the Report

This report aims to highlight the impact of I-Corps-trained teams, individuals, and companies affiliated with Great Lakes region universities. The teams included participated

 in either a program offered by the NSF I-Corps Hub: Great Lakes Region or the NSF I-Corps™ Teams program between 2012 and 2025.

Data was collected from sources, including, but not limited to, Pitchbook, the Great Lakes I-Corps Hub database, the VentureWell database, and federal funding databases.

### About the NSF I-Corps Hub: Great Lakes Region

The NSF I-Corps Hub: Great Lakes Region aims to spur innovation, collaboration, and economic impact in the

 region. Supported by a grant from the National Science Foundation, this regional alliance of 16+ Universities

 educates, supports, and connects academic researchers to the entrepreneurial ecosystem across the Midwest.

This work is supported by the NSF I-Corps Hub: Great Lakes Region, NSF Award #2048612
