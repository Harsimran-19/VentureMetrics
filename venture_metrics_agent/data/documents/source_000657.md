## ABOUT US

Established in 1887, the Engineering Institute of Canada (EIC) is Canada’s longest-standing engineering society and now a federation of 15 learned engineering societies. The EIC promotes strong continuing education standards, recognizes meritorious engineers, records Canadian engineering history, holds thematic conferences and provides a policy advisory voice to governments through the Partnership Group for Science and Engineering (PAGSE). Its charitable EIC FUND aims to support engineering excellence and the annual EIC Awards Gala.

Tap into Canada's largest and most dedicated engineering job board for finding exciting career opportunities. With over 10 society partners we bring the best opportunities right to you.
