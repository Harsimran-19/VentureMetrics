#### Promoting and fostering professionalism

HKPEFA aims to promote the private equity and venture capital industry in Hong Kong and to act as the connecting arm with Associations in Mainland China and the Asian region. It is to provide a platform for finance and related professionals and practitioners to communicate on issues, policies and guidelines affecting the industry with the ultimate aim to promote Hong Kong as a private equity hub in the Greater China region

#### Consultation Body

One of HKPEFA's initiatives is to enable technical discussion on policies affecting the Hong Kong private equity and venture capital industry and to liaise and advise governmental agencies on matters concerning the development of the industry

#### Knowledge Sharing

HKPEFA aims to provide education and training to finance and related professionals in the private equity and venture capital industry in Hong Kong, to enable continual growth in the technicality of the professions
