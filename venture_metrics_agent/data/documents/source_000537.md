# Understanding the UK's high-growth artificial intelligence companies

![](https://www.beauhurst.com/wp-content/uploads/2023/12/Understanding-AI-companies-report-cover-1024x576.png)

**For the fourth instalment in our report series with Barclays Eagle Labs, we’ve done an in-depth analysis of the dynamic and rapidly changing artificial intelligence sector in the UK.**

AI is more relevant now than ever, and its rapid evolution has been a significant driver of various company exits and substantial investment into the sector. This report examines the 1,797 active, high-growth AI companies in the UK. It delves into the geographical clusters that define the landscape of this industry, including the concentration of over 57% of these organisations within London.

Equity investment plays a vital role in fostering growth and supporting innovation in high-growth AI companies. In 2022 alone, these companies secured an unprecedented £3.21b in equity funding.
