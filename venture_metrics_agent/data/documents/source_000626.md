## Hot Occupations 

![Image 37](blob:http://localhost/7e8eb0e97cb784286aeb1e474646069e)

![Image 38: Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Executives](https://cspoprdweb.azureedge.net/assets/image/Original/4365.jpeg)

#### [Customer Relationship Management (CRM) Executives](javascript:;)

Maintain sound customer relations, interact with customers to handle enquiries and complaints, process orders, prepare reports and receive visitors.

![Image 39: Lecturers/ Professors](https://cspoprdweb.azureedge.net/assets/image/Original/4244.jpeg)

#### [Lecturers/ Professors](javascript:;)

Lecturers/ Professors prepare and deliver lectures and conduct tutorials in a wide variety of professional, academic and technical subjects within a prescribed course of study at a university or higher education institutions. They conduct research, and prepare scholarly papers and books.

![Image 40: Social Media Influencers](https://cspoprdweb.azureedge.net/assets/image/Original/8126.jpeg)

#### [Social Media Influencers](javascript:;)

Social Media Influencers are individuals with large fan bases who provide new product recommendations in their niches through videos or live broadcasting, photos and articles on social media platforms. They are Key Opinion Leaders (KOLs) among their followers in various fields, including food, travel, fashion, beauty, electronic products, kitchenware, electronic games, luxury goods, etc.

![Image 41: Construction Managers](https://cspoprdweb.azureedge.net/assets/image/Original/4182.jpeg)

#### [Construction Managers](javascript:;)

Construction Managers coordinate and supervise a wide variety of construction projects, including the building of all types of public, residential, commercial, and industrial structures, as well as roads and bridges. Although they oversee the whole construction projects, they may consult with developers and builders on construction related issues.
