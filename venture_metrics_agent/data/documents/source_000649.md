### Apply and get feedback in 30 days only with Job Bank

With a Job Bank Plus account, you get updates on the status of your application within 30 days of applying. Find out if your application was retained or not, or if the job was filled or has expired.

[Start now](https://www.jobbank.gc.ca/reg/instructions)

Thousands of summer jobs are now available across the country for young Canadians. Don’t miss out!

[Search Canada Summer Jobs](https://www.jobbank.gc.ca/youth)

Apply in one click and get matched with employers with a Job Bank Plus account. Sign up for free to customize your preferences, save jobs and track your activity.
