## How is Student Circus different from other job portals?

Student Circus is a niche job search portal for international students in the UK who are currently on the Student Route visa (earlier known as the Tier-4 visa). We filter opportunities from companies that sponsor a work visa for international students. Student Circus helps in saving an insane amount of time that International Students end up wasting by applying for irrelevant jobs.

## Who can sign-up to use Student Circus?

All students studying in UK universities can use this service to find suitable jobs.

## Can I use Student Circus if my university is NOT a partner?

Yes! If your university isn’t a partner yet, you can still explore Student Circus by signing up with your university email ID. This will give you free 14-day trial access to the platform. Please note that, in some cases, access may be restricted for certain universities.

## Can I extend my free trial after 14 days?

Yes. You can request an additional 14 days of free access by referring Student Circus to your university’s career services. To do so, please send an email to your career services team explaining how Student Circus has been beneficial to you, and copy hello@studentcircus.com in the email. As a token of appreciation, we will extend your trial by a further 14 days.
