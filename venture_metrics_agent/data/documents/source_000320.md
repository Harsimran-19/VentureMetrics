## Our Criteria

![Image 12: Innovative,](https://www.jaree.sa/assets/imgs/Group%2090.png)

## Innovative,

Tech-Driven Model

We prioritize startups that leverage technology to build disruptive business models, setting themselves apart with unique solutions that enhance operational efficiency and create competitive advantages.

![Image 13: Experienced,](https://www.jaree.sa/assets/imgs/Business%20Deal%201.png)

## Experienced,

Complementary Team

A strong founding team with diverse expertise in key areas such as technology, business strategy, marketing, and finance is essential. We value founders with a proven track record, including previous successful exits.

![Image 14: Demonstrated](https://www.jaree.sa/assets/imgs/Group%2091.png)

## Demonstrated

Traction

Preference is given to startups that have secured commitments from VCs or reputable angel investors, and those showcasing consistent growth, such as maintaining over 12% month-on-month growth over the past 12 months.

![Image 15: High Growth](https://www.jaree.sa/assets/imgs/Money%20Safe%202%201.png)

## High Growth

Market Potential

Startups should target large and growing markets, addressing significant challenges or needs that signal strong revenue potential and an opportunity for substantial market share.

![Image 16: Saudi Founding](https://www.jaree.sa/assets/imgs/Group%2093.png)

## Saudi Founding

Team Member

A minimum of one founder must be of Saudi nationality, ensuring a solid understanding of the local market and fostering strong ties within the Saudi ecosystem.
