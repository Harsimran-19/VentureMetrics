# Global Innovation Index 2025

Tracking the most recent global innovation trends, the GII captures a moment of recalibration where R&D growth has slowed, venture capital activity has moderated with cautious early-stage funding, and patenting gains remain modest, even as collaboration to address shared global challenges evolves through networked ecosystems and multi-stakeholder platforms.

The Global Innovation Index 2025 reveals innovation systems at a crossroads, where breakthrough technologies in AI and quantum computing advance rapidly while investment growth slows and collaborative models evolve to address global challenges. To drill further into the data behind the GII, visit the [GII Innovation Ecosystems & Data Explorer](https://www.wipo.int/gii-ranking/en/).
