THRIVING ENTREPRENEURIAL SPIRIT

*   Israel invests heavily in education and research, spending 4.38% of its GDP into R&D, the highest percentage in the world [1].
*   Israel is ranked 3rd in the world for the quality of its scientific institutions [2].
*   Israel is ranked 1st in the world for innovative capacity and 1st in the world for entrepreneurship [3]. Israel has the highest concentration of high-tech companies in the world, apart from Silicon Valley.

![Image 5: IsraelTechGate](https://israeltechgate.com/images/globe_ITG.png)

PIONEERING TECHNOLOGIES

*   Israel is ranked 1st in the world for its central bank policy and 4th for the resilience of its economy [5].
*   Israel is ranked 6th for investor protection out of 148 economies [6].
*   In 2009, as most of the world experienced economic crisis, Israel saw a 1.1% GDP growth and by the third quarter of 2013 had already leaped to 4.9% [7]. 

![Image 6: IsraelTechGate](https://israeltechgate.com/images/globe_ITG.png)

RESILIENT ECONOMY and GOVERNMENT SUPPORT

*   The close ties between academia, industry, and government enable scientific innovation to be swiftly translated into profitable business initiatives. Israel reaches $25B in technological exports annually [4].
*   With 1000+ of innovation startups per year Israel is leading the future of business in: Media & Internet, Cyber Security, WaterTech, AgriTech, Health & Life Sciences, FinTech and other sectors.
*   In 2015 Israeli startups generated $7.2B in M&A. 

![Image 7: IsraelTechGate](https://israeltechgate.com/images/globe_ITG.png)

INVESTMENT OPPORTUNITIES

*   300+ multinational companies such as Microsoft, Motorola, Google, Apple, Facebook, Berkshire-Hathaway, Intel, HP, Siemens, GE, IBM, Toshiba and Cisco use Israel as an international hub for their innovation, operations and investment.
*   Israel's flourishing venture capital market totals $1B [4]. Israel is ranked 9th for venture capital availability out of 148 economies [6].
*   State of Israel encourages international and local investment by offering grants and tax related benefits.
