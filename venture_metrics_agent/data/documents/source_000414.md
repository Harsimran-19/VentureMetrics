# EY Entrepreneurship Barometer 2025

[![Image 2](https://ami.cname.ro/1024_768/ey-entrepreneurship-barometer-2025/article/mediaPool/uFZyqtu.jpg)](https://www.amcham.ro/news-from-members/ey-entrepreneurship-barometer-2025)

**by EY February 21, 2025**

**Website**[www.ey.com](https://www.ey.com/)

[](https://www.facebook.com/dialog/share?app_id=296519667211701&href=https://www.amcham.ro/news-from-members/ey-entrepreneurship-barometer-2025&display=popup)[](https://twitter.com/share?url=https://www.amcham.ro/news-from-members/ey-entrepreneurship-barometer-2025&text=)

Entrepreneurship is at the heart of economic progress, shaping industries, driving innovation, and creating opportunities. But today’s business leaders are navigating an increasingly complex world, one where rapid technological advancements, economic shifts, and geopolitical uncertainties test their resilience.

The **EY Entrepreneurship Barometer 2025** is designed to provide a clearer picture of a continuously evolving landscape, offering real insights from entrepreneurs across Central, Eastern, Southeastern Europe including Romania.

This survey explores what is truly shaping business success today. **Innovation and digital transformation** are more than just buzzwords, they are vital for companies looking to stay ahead, with AI, automation, and cloud technologies reshaping industries. At the same time, access to capital and investment challenges stay front and center, influencing growth strategies. The survey also highlights workforce trends, from talent retention to shifting expectations of employees in a hybrid world.

Beyond individual business concerns, we take a wider view examining the **entrepreneurial ecosystem**, including regulatory challanges, infrastructure gaps, and access to global markets. Family businesses, a backbone of many economies, also get special attention, particularly around succession planning and long-term sustainability.

By revealing these key trends, EY aims to empower entrepreneurs with actionable insights to navigate uncertainty, seize opportunities, and drive sustainable, long-term success.
