# The [Global Entrepreneurship Congress](http://www.gec.co/?utm_source=StartupCouncil)is returning to the US in June!

## Want to join us?

**The [Global Entrepreneurship Congress](http://www.gec.co/?utm_source=StartupCouncil)is the world’s pre-eminent global convocation of innovation experts.**

Thousands of startup founders, government policy-makers, investors, ecosystem builders, and friends from all over the world are convening June 2-5, 2025 in the US city of Indianapolis, Indiana.

After successful events in Riyadh, Melbourne, Istanbul, Shanghai, Rio de Janiero, Milan and more, this is the first time in 15 years that the [GEC](http://www.gec.co/?utm_source=StartupCouncil)is returning to the United States!

The world's boldest entrepreneurs, investors, policymakers and ecosystem builders from 200 countries + territories are landing in Indiana, USA June 2-5, 2025 for one of the most impactful and influential live connections of global entrepreneurship ecosystem players. Join us!

## World Class Speakers & Workshops

**GEC 2025 is pleased to welcome hundreds of noted global experts in entrepreneurship, investment, innovation policy, and ecosystem building.**

These speakers include:

* **Mark Cuban**: Shark Tank reality TV star, serial entrepreneur and startup investor, sports team owner, and graduate of Indiana University. As GEC2025’s Keynote Speaker, Mr. Cuban will draw upon his own decades of success as an entrepreneur to share insights with the GEC audience.
* **Jeff Hoffman**: Global Humanitarian and Chairman of the Board of GEN, award-winning global entrepreneur, proven CEO, worldwide motivational speaker, Jeff has been part of a number of well-known successful startups, including Priceline.com/Booking.com, uBid.com, and more.
* **Brad Feld**: Pioneering tech investor, co-founder of the Techstars Accelerators, best-selling author of Venture Deals, Startup Communities, and more.
* **Scott Fox, CEO of StartupCouncil.org**: Global angel investor, best-selling entrepreneurship author, innovation ecosystem leader, early stage fundraising expert speaker, and startup YouTuber.

And hundreds more, with new speakers being added daily!  ([See the Agenda for Global Entrepreneurship Congress 2025 here](http://www.gec.co/?utm_source=StartupCouncil).)

## What to Expect at GEC: A Fusion of Ideas and Action

The [Global Entrepreneurship Congress](http://www.gec.co/?utm_source=StartupCouncil), hosted annually by the Global Entrepreneurship Network (GEN) in a dynamic entrepreneurial city, is more than just a gathering; it's a catalyst. Over four transformative days, attendees will:

* Be Inspired: Thought leaders will challenge conventional thinking and illuminate emerging trends and opportunities.
* Shape Policy: Government officials will engage in the Startup Nations Ministerial, crafting policies that ignite new firm formation.
* Gain Insights: Academics will share data-driven insights into the performance of entrepreneurship programs and ecosystems.
* Find New Deals: Investors will meet entrepreneurs from all over the world to explore cutting-edge new investment opportunities.
* Scale Your Vision: Startups will connect with experienced mentors and investors, gaining invaluable guidance for growth.
* Build Your Network: All participants will forge lasting connections, expanding their influence across global innovation ecosystems.

## Who Should Attend?

The [Global Entrepreneurship Congress](http://www.gec.co/?utm_source=StartupCouncil)is a fun, informative, and inspiring event. 4 days of top quality speakers, sessions, networking and social events.

Everyone is welcome! Attend GEC 2025 to meet thousands of fellow:

* Entrepreneurs and Startup Founders
* Angel and Venture Capital Investors
* Elected Officials and Government Policy Makers
* Ecosystem Development Professionals
* Academics
