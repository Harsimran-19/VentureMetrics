### 2 Funding Schemes 1 Direction

The below 2 funding schemes lead to 1 direction, HK$600,000 funding support to kick start your startup journey.

​

EASE Fund

The Education⁺ and Social Entrepreneurs (EASE) Fund Scheme, is a seed funding scheme set up by the Knowledge Transfer Sub-office (KT Sub-office) of The Education University of Hong Kong, aims to create and promote a culture of innovation and entrepreneurship in EdUHK community; to support students and alumni to pursue their dreams and creations by starting up a sustainable venture; and to provide a new channel to transfer EdUHK knowledge and research outcomes into real applications to benefit the society.

​

Subsidized by the Youth Development Commission, the EASE Fund 2024/25 is enhanced with the support of the Funding Scheme for Youth Entrepreneurship in the Guangdong - Hong Kong - Macao Greater Bay Area under the Home and Youth Affairs Bureau (HYAB).

​​​IP Venture Scheme

Meanwhile, the IP Venture Scheme was first initiated by KTSO in 2025, which complements the EASE Fund by facilitating the transfer of knowledge and EdUHK's research outcomes into real-life applications that benefit society.

Through this Kick-off event, we aim to bring together government officials, EdUHK academics, and the entrepreneurial community to create synergy and forge meaningful connections.​​
